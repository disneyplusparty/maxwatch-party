How to Transform Movie Nights with Max Watch Party?

Hosting a Max Party is a cinch with these simple steps: The Max Watch Party Chrome extension is incredibly user-friendly, ensuring everyone can join the fun. Don't wait any longer—kick off your virtual movie night with these easy instructions:

  • Max Watch Party Installation To get started, click here to install the Max Watch Party extension. It works seamlessly on Chromebook, macOS, and Windows devices and is compatible with both Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge browsers.
  • Pin the Extension to Your Toolbar For quick access, pin the Max Watch Party Chrome extension to your toolbar. Navigate to the “Extensions” icon in the top-right corner of your browser, and click the pin button next to the Max Watch Party extension.
  • Max Party Sign-In Log into your Max Party account to start or join a watch party and watch your favorite content with friends.
  • Play the Video Log into your Max Party account, search for the video you want to watch, and press play. Enjoy sharing the viewing experience with your watch party, making every moment more enjoyable together.
  • Create a Max Party To become the watch party host, click the “PLAY” button in the toolbar. This action will open the “Create a Party” window, where you can choose the “Start a Party” option. You can decide whether you want to have control over the watch party by toggling the “Only I have control” button on or off. Once done, a watch party URL will be generated for you to share with friends and invite them to join the fun.
  • Join the Party First, install the extension and log in to your Max Party account to join a Max Party. Then, click the Watch Party URL shared by your friend to enter the party and enjoy the show together.
  • Join the Party To participate in a Max Party, begin by installing the Max Watch Party Chrome extension and logging into your Max Party account. Next, click the Watch Party URL sent by your friend, and you'll be seamlessly joined into the party to enjoy the show together.
